definition: the German translation of the English words 'all', 'everything', and 'anything'

pronounciation: all-Ehs

What is alles?


alles was made to simplify some minor annoyances in life like opening
all the needed files or websites everytime you have to work on
an assignment, test, etc.

alles lets you:
-create personalized scripts/macros
-convert YouTube videos to mp3s
-download YouTube videos
-email with ease
-Google/YouTube fast

Why?

Have a family member who's slow with technology? alles won't be able to magically transform people into the next Bill Gates of computers, but hopefully it can make simplify the process for some people by simplifying tasks and also provide convenience.
